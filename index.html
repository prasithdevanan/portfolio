About Me

I’m Prasith Devanan, a UI/UX and Graphic Designer with over 2 years of experience creating web, mobile, and AR/VR designs. I enjoy turning ideas into easy-to-use digital products using tools like Figma and Adobe XD. With a background in branding and visual design, I bring a complete approach — from concept to final product.
